Pilot Timeline
Week-by-Week Execution Plan
Four phases covering setup, live detection, engagement measurement, and final reporting — structured to produce a scale/no-scale decision memo at day 30.
Days 1–5 — Pre-Launch
Environment Configuration & Data Onboarding
Confirm pilot population selection (100–500 households). Establish secure AMI feed (CSV or API, daily at 02:00). Load GIS parcel layer with zoning, reuse zone, and HOA boundary data. Configure ordinance rules (ORD 82.2 schedules, drought stage baseline, exemptions). Establish role-based access for analyst, enforcement, and admin lanes. Deploy PWA to 50 volunteer cohort.
AMI Feed Setup GIS Load Ordinance Config PWA Deploy RBAC
Days 6–10 — Week 1 Live
First Ingestion Cycle & Baseline Validation
Execute first full ingestion cycle. Validate schema, deduplication, and timestamp alignment on pilot parcel set. Confirm 95.3% compliance tracking accuracy on sample. Review work queue classification — wrong-day, overwatering risk, anomaly flags. Log first batch of simulated violations to validate automated SR logic. Capture ingestion success rate and queue backlog metrics.
Ingestion Validation Accuracy Benchmark Work Queue Review Simulated SRs
Days 11–20 — Weeks 2–3
Customer Engagement & Behavior Measurement
Issue targeted outreach to flagged households — education notices, ordinance guidance, PWA tips. Open 7–14 day response window per case. Monitor PWA engagement signals from 50-volunteer cohort. Track wrong-day violation rate in test group vs. control group. Capture verification uploads (rain sensor photo confirmations). Re-check compliance after outreach window closes — confirm case closure or escalation routing.
Outreach Delivery PWA Engagement Response Window Behavior Recheck Verification Upload
Days 21–25 — Week 4
Enforcement Workflow & Escalation Testing
Route non-compliant cases to enforcement lane. Validate penalty logic against ordinance ruleset. Confirm audit-ready documentation output. Test access control checks and audit log review. Capture staff and volunteer feedback via structured survey. Review dispute handling workflow on any contested cases.
Penalty Logic Audit Log Review Access Control Test Staff Feedback
Days 26–30 — Close-Out
Results Memo & Scale Decision
Produce pilot results memo: accuracy rate, violation reduction delta, engagement metrics, uptime log, and enforcement throughput. Finalize interface documentation — schemas and sample payloads. Present scale/no-scale recommendation with supporting KPI data. Deliver hardening recommendations for full-scale deployment scope.
Results Memo Schema Docs Scale Decision KPI Package
